About Me

Earth Scientist Passionate About Climate Solutions

I am a recent graduate with an Integrated BS-MS in Geology from IISER Bhopal (May 2026), specialising in CO₂ sequestration in basaltic formations. My thesis involved developing and validating a 1D reactive transport model using Python-PHREEQC to simulate CO₂ mineralisation, porewater chemistry evolution, and porosity-permeability coupling.

With hands-on experience in seismic data analysis, GIS, and machine learning, I bring an interdisciplinary approach to geoscience problems. I am deeply passionate about advancing subsurface climate solutions, and I am actively seeking advanced industry roles or PhD opportunities where I can apply my computational modelling and geoscientific expertise.
